ONE TASTE, TWO WORLDS.

MEXICO AND JAPAN. Two cultures, one passion for flavor. At **Sinalove**, we fuse the vibrant cuisine of Sinaloa with the delicacy of Japanese tradition. We celebrate the joy of flavor and harmonious coexistence.

GOURMET TACOS

Our tacos are an ode to the flavor of Sinaloa, with options ranging from the classic **Asada** to our special seafood tacos. Each tortilla is perfectly heated, served with homemade sauces with the perfect touch of heat.

*Ensenada Shrimp Taco, Governor Taco, Citrus Fish Taco.*

FUSION SUSHI

The delicacy of sushi with a Mexican twist. From the **Sinaloense Roll** with tempura shrimp and cream cheese, to the **Spicy Tuna with Chipotle**. An explosion of flavor that respects Japanese technique.

*Culiacán Roll, Serrano Sashimi, Nigiris with Ponzu Sauce.*

HANDCRAFTED, WITH LOVE.

From the perfectly seasoned sushi rice to the marinade for the taco meat. We pour our passion into every detail. This is our commitment: **a labor of love for gastronomy.**
